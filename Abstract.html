Abstract

Our Project deals with the Android Phone usage by different peoples in various Villages and the types of
phone usage and to get awareness among the people and to get their life firster and smarter by the use of
New Android Phone technologies and upgrade their life's by the upgrading technology.

In this Project, We Explore the Android Phone usage of various age group of people and to know how they
are concentrated by their Android phone quality and type of company.Through this project we can know
Android phone usage of different generations of the people. Our Project will not only shed's the light on the
diverse ways people engage with Android Phones but also highlight the importance of considering user
diversity in the development and marketing of mobile Applications.
